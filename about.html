SHON BEN-ITZHAK

Israeli Attorney & Notary

About Attorney Shon Ben-Itzhak Office Profile Contact

Attorney at Law Shon Ben-Itzhak has a LL.B. degree and was admitted as an attorney by the Israeli Bar Association.

Shon has extensive experience and is very successful in his office practices.

Shon is a graduate of a military academy, a Major in the Israeli Air Force. He served ten years in the army and later in the reserved forces.

Shon holds a "commercial pilot license" on multi-engine aircrafts. In his past, he worked as a commercial pilot and managed two aviation companies in Israel.

Additionally, Shon is a landscape architect and is a certified "director of companies".

Shon has been residing with his family in California, USA, since 2017.
